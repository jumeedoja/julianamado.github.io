City of Oakland Towing Codes

General City of Oakland Towed Vehicle Codes

The City of Oakland, in Accordance with California State Law, has set the following towing codes:

22651A: Vehicle Left Unattended On A Bridge, Viaduct or Causeway

22651B: Vehicle Obstructing or Blocking Traffic on a Public Street

22651C: Stolen/Emblezzed Vehicle

22651D: Vehicle Blocking A Driveway

22651E: Vehicle Blocking A Fire Hydrant

22651F: Vehicle Left Abandoned On A Freeway

22651G: Driver Incapacitated Due To Injury or Illness

22651H: Driver In Custody Due To An Arrest

22651I: Five Or More Outstanding Parking Tickets

22651J: No Proof of Insurance for a Vehicle

22651K: Parked on a Public Street for More than 72 Hours

22651L: Construction Zone

22651M: Movement of Large Objects

22651N: Tow Away Zone

22651O: Expired Registration over 6 Months

22651P: Expired Driver's License

23102(A): Vehilce Involved in Side-Show Activity (30 Day Hold)

14602.6A: Suspended Driver's License (30 Day Hold)

Vehicle Releases are provided to the registered owner(s) on DMV file.

A vehicle release is $166 payable in cash, VISA, or Mastercard.

Vehicle releases can only be given to the registered owners on file with DMV.
